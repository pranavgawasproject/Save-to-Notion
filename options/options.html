How to Connect

  1. Click "Connect to Notion" button above
  2. Authorize the extension in the Notion popup
  3. Select which pages/databases to share
  4. You're all set! Start clipping research

Note: We only access the pages you explicitly share. Your data is stored locally and never sent to our servers.
